    class ReadUntil::ReadCache

    Overview

    Thread-safe bounded cache keyed by channel.

    Keeps the most recent chunk per channel up to size total entries. When full, the oldest entry (by insertion order) is evicted. .missed — reads evicted before consumption (distinct read replaced). .replaced — chunks overwritten by a later chunk of the same read.
